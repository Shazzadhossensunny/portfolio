HotelHaven

This page contains the case study of HotelHaven Open-Source Project which includes the Project Overview, Tools Used and Live Links to the official product.

Live Link

Project Overview

HotelHaven is a comprehensive hotel room booking platform designed to streamline the reservation process and enhance user experience. Users can easily book rooms for their desired dates, update their bookings, and even cancel them (with a one-day cancellation policy). Once a room is booked, its status is immediately updated to unavailable, ensuring real-time availability tracking.

The room page provides users with an overview of all available and unavailable rooms, allowing them to filter rooms based on price to find the perfect accommodation within their budget. Additionally, users who have booked rooms can leave reviews to share their experiences, helping other potential guests make informed decisions.

HotelHaven aims to simplify the hotel booking process, providing users with a seamless and efficient platform to find and reserve accommodations for their travel needs.

Core Features

  • Users can book an available room
  • Users can review their own booked room
  • Users can update their booking date
